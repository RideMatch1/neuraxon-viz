Interactive Anna Explorer

Explore words, sentences, and communication patterns discovered in the Anna Matrix. Think of it like a dictionary and phrase book - search for specific words, see which ones appear most often, and understand how Anna might communicate. All data comes from systematic analysis of the 128×128 matrix structure.

What you'll find here: Word frequency charts, sentence patterns, and a visual heatmap showing where words appear in the grid structure. All data is extracted from the same Anna Matrix that led to discovering 23,765 Qubic identities. Ongoing research: We're actively discovering words with 4, 5, 6 letters and longer patterns. This page shows our current findings.

What is this? Analysis of the Anna Matrix revealed that certain words and sentence patterns appear repeatedly, indicating organized communication. This page lets you explore those patterns through interactive charts and searchable lists.

ℹ
